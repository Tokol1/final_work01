• Pyro Slime

    Pyro Slimes are monsters in Genshin Impact. They are a type of Slime that can be found in the wilds of Teyvat. They are weak to Hydro attacks, but resistant to Pyro attacks.

  • Pyro Regisvine

    Pyro Regisvine is a monster in Genshin Impact. It is a giant plant that can be found in the wilds of Teyvat. It is weak to Cryo attacks, but resistant to Pyro attacks.
